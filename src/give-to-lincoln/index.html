Give To Lincoln Day

Give to Girls Code Lincoln during the month of May and all donations will be eligible for a match from the Lincoln Community Foundation! Think of it as the cherry on top; not only are you giving, but the more we raise before May 30th, the more Girls Code Lincoln gets as part of Give to Lincoln Day! Every penny counts!

Give To Lincoln Day, By Check

During the Month of May, make checks payable to the Lincoln Community Foundation and note Girls Code Lincoln in the memo line to ensure that they are eligible for the match. Checks must be received at the Lincoln Community Foundation by 5pm on May 30th.
